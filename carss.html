The Cyber Automotive Risk Scoring System (CARSS) is a tool that helps assess and measure cybersecurity risks in automotive systems. Following ISO/SAE 21434 standard, CARSS offers a clear and consistent way to evaluate and score cyber risks.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive Risk Assessment
  • Based on ISO/SAE 21434
  • CAL Value, Attack Feasibility & Risk Value Analysis
  • User-Friendly Interface
  • Real-Time Scoring System
  • Customizable Risk Parameters

Why Choose CARSS?

  • Speed up your risk calculation during TARA
  • Provides a structured and consistent risk assessment
  • Helps in complying with industry standards
  • Facilitates informed decision-making on risk management
  • Improves vehicle security against cyber threats
  • Suitable for cybersecurity professionals, manufacturers, and suppliers
